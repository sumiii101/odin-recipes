Lasanga

Description of dish

This is an italian dish that consists of pasta sheets, mince meat in a rich tomato sauce, and a thick creamy cheese sauce

Steps

Ingredients needed:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mince meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Meat seasonings
  • White sauce
  • Mozzerella sauce

What to do:

  1. Cook your mince meet with onions add seasonings
  2. Add your tomato sauce and cook on medium head for 20 mins
  3. Boil your lasana sheets in boiling water until soft
  4. Now add a layer of your cooked mince meat to a square pan
  5. Add a layer of lasagna sheets to pan
  6. Add a layer of mince meat followed by white sauce then add pasta sheets again
  7. Repeat until all Ingredients are used and add Mozzerella cheese on top
  8. Bake for 20 mins
