Buldak creamy noodles

Description of dish

The creamy spicy noodles you always see on tiktok!

Steps

Ingredients needed:

  • Double cream
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Samyang buldak carbona noodles
  • Oil
  • Egg(optional)

What to do:

  1. First, boil your noodles until theyre cooked
  2. add 1 tbsp of oil into a pan
  3. Add your noodles along with around 4 tbsp of noodle water
  4. Add the whole packet of sauce packet
  5. Mix thoroughly and add 4 tbsp of double cream
  6. Add Mozerella cheese to liking and mix throughly
  7. In a seperate pan, crack an egg and cook
  8. Pour noodles into bowl and add egg on top
