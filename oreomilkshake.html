Oreo Milkshake

Description of dish

A cold dessert drink perfect for summer for oreo lovers

Steps

Ingredients needed:

  • Packet of regular oreos
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Whole milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream

What to do:

  1. Crush up oreos using rolling pin
  2. Add 2 cups of milk to a blender
  3. Next add 4 scoops of Vanilla ice cream
  4. Add a cup of the oreo crumps
  5. Next add 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  6. Blend all the ingdredients until fully combined
  7. Pour mixture into a cup and add whipped cream on top
  8. Add whole oreo on top and you're done!
